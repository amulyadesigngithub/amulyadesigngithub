ABOUT US

  • Amulya Designs is a leading architectural and interior design firm dedicated to meeting all the needs of its clients while imparting a unique identity to each space. With a diverse clientele spanning the hospitality, residential, and commercial sectors, we have successfully completed over 200 interior design projects in India and abroad, providing tailored solutions for each.

  • Our firm specializes in offering contemporary, innovative, and functional design solutions for residential, commercial, and F&B projects. With expertise in both architecture and interiors, we also develop in-house innovative product designs to enhance every project's uniqueness.
